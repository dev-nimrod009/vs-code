How to prepare Sausage Spaghetti

ingridients

one packet spaghetti,
one packet Sausages,
coriander,
Cayenne,
Blackpepper,
Tomato paste,
Garlic,
Blended tomato,
paprika powder
and Dania Garnishings.

Instructions

The following are the Instructions

  • Fry the Sausages
  • Boil Spaghetti.Drain the water after it is well cooked
  • wash the spaghetti with cold water
  • Oil the pan and fry the onions and garlic powder till they are golden brown
  • Add blended tomatoes,tomato paste and spices.Allow it to cook well.
  • Add the spaghetti in the cooked paste and stir till it gets reddish.
  • After stirring the mixture ,add the sausages which has been cut into small pieces and stir again till it's well mixed.
  • Garnish with dahnia

yields

Can be served to a family of about 6 people
